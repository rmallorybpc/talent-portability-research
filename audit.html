Talent Portability Research

Audit

What this synthesis can and cannot claim, with open verification checks.

What this synthesis can claim

  • Buyer-side overpayment appears repeatedly, and experience does not reliably correct it.
  • Star performance is contextual more often than portable.
  • Talent transfer improves when the value-creating asset is portable.
  • The sports findings and business literature converge directionally across independent datasets.

What this synthesis cannot claim

  • It cannot claim causal certainty across all included findings.
  • It cannot fully separate effort effects from context effects in post-move declines.
  • It cannot generalize uniformly across all industries and role types.
  • It cannot infer where all transition-lost assets ultimately go.
  • It cannot claim outside hiring never works.
